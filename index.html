Benchmark References

The cases below are grouped by standard validation families: exact homogeneous Boltzmann relaxation, free-molecular wall flows, molecular aperture/effusion laws, collisionless transport, Riemann fans, ballistic phase mixing, and Maxwell gas-surface reflection.

[BKW] Krook and Wu, Exact solutions of the Boltzmann equation; the classical Bobylev-Krook-Wu homogeneous Maxwell-molecule solution family.

[RGD] Cercignani, Rarefied Gas Dynamics; canonical rarefied-flow settings including free-molecular flow, Couette/Fourier slabs, and thermal-transpiration effects.

[WALL] Maxwell diffuse-specular boundary model; gas-surface reflection split by the accommodation coefficient.

[PLATE] Free-molecular flat-plate loading; standard incidence-angle and speed-sweep checks for rarefied aerodynamics.

[APERTURE] NIST pinhole-orifice conductance measurements and molecular-flow conductance references for aperture fluxes.

[EFFUSION] Molecular effusion and reservoir boundary formulation; flux-weighted Maxwellian emission through small openings.

[RAYLEIGH] Kinetic Rayleigh problem; impulsively moved flat wall in kinetic/free-molecular limits.

[TRANSPORT] Collisionless/free-transport kinetic theory; characteristic propagation, absorbing/specular walls, and exact moment transport.

[PHASE] Mouhot and Villani, On Landau damping; phase mixing as velocity-space shearing around free-transport dynamics.

How to read the error numbers

Max error is the largest absolute difference between the computed benchmark quantities and the public exact/reference values for that case. A smaller number means closer agreement.

Worst max error is the largest card-level max error on this page. P95 max error means 95% of the listed cases are at or below that error level. Median max error is the middle case after sorting by error.

Decimal digits is an approximate readability label: an error near 1e-13 corresponds to about 13 decimal digits of agreement. Values shown as 0.00e+0 mean the rounded public statistic is at floating-point display precision for the checked quantities.
